PowerBi

DATA-DRIVEN SUCCESS

Power BI is a business analytics tool developed by Microsoft. It provides interactive visualizations with self-service business intelligence capabilities, where end users can create reports and dashboards by themselves, without having to depend on information technology staff or database administrators. Power BI provides cloud-based BI services, known as "Power BI Services", along with a desktop based interface, called "Power BI Desktop". It offers data warehouse capabilities including data preparation, data discovery and interactive dashboards.

Advantages of Power BI:

  • Rich personalized dashboards
  • The ability to leverage existing Excel reports
  • Quick Insights which allows you to explore your data and find patterns you may not have otherwise found
  • Natural Language Query which allows you to ask questions and get answers

Disadvantages of Power BI:

  • Power BI is dependent on Microsoft Excel and SQL
  • Limited data source connectors
  • Complex pricing model

Power BI User Guidelines

Millions of data professionals use Power BI

Whether you're a data analyst, business decision maker, or a data enthusiast, Power BI is your tool. It's a powerful business analytics service provided by Microsoft. It provides interactive visualizations with self-service business intelligence capabilities.

Power BI is a collection of software services, apps, and connectors that work together to turn unrelated sources of data into coherent, visually immersive, and interactive insights.

Creating a Power BI Report:

  • Sign in to your Power BI account.
  • On the left navigation pane, select "Workspace".
  • Click on the "Create" button and select "Report".
  • Choose the dataset for your report.
  • Use the "Fields", "Visualizations", and "Filters" panes to design your report.
  • Click "File" > "Save" to save your report.

Sharing a Power BI Report:

  • Open the report you want to share.
  • Click on the "Share" button at the top right corner.
  • Enter the email addresses of the people you want to share the report with.
  • Click "Share".
